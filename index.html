Tic Tac Toe

Game Overview

Tic Tac Toe is one of the most popular strategy games. It is played on a 3×3 grid where two players take turns marking X and O. The first player to align three symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

Game Highlights

Easy to Learn

Simple rules make it beginner-friendly.

Strategic Thinking

Players must predict opponent moves.

Classic Game

Played worldwide for generations.

How to Get Started

  1. Player X starts first.
  2. Click an empty square.
  3. Take turns with your opponent.
  4. Get three symbols in a row to win.
  5. Press Restart to play again.
